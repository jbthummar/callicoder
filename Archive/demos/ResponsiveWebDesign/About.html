    • About Us

    Originally from Vancouver, British Columbia, Dr Mawdsley moved to Ontario after high school to attend Queen's University. After graduating with a Bachelor of Science (Honors) in Life Sciences, he returned to Vancouver in 1991 to attend Medical School at UBC. He then went on to complete a five year residency at Dalhousie University in Obstetrics and Gynecology. In 2000, he joined the faculty at Dalhousie University where he is currently an Assistant Professor in the department of Obstetrics and Gynecology.

    Dr Mawdsley practices Obstetrics and Gynecology in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia. He provides gynecologic surgery at the Dartmouth General Hospital with urgent obstetric care and deliveries at the IWK Health Centre. He has a special interest in minimally invasive gynecologic surgery and hormone replacement options.
