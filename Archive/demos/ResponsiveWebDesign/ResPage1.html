    • Contact Us

    Our office is located in Dartmouth, Nova Scotia. Please click on the address to view a goople map and for directions.

    We welcome and encourage you to contact the office if you have a problem, a question, or need to get in touch with Dr Mawdsley for any reason. Because Dr Mawdsley’s time is usually scheduled, the receptionist will either schedule an appointment or arrange for a time when Dr Mawdsley or his nurse can return your call.

    If you would like to change an appointment time or confirm an appointment, you are welcome to do so by email. Since we cannot guarantee email privacy or security, email can only be used for basic scheduling purposes at your initiation. Please do not include any confidential information and do not use email for anything urgent.

    If the office is closed or the receptionist is unavailable please leave a confidential message on our voice mail. Please spell you name and give your date of birth so we can locate your medical record. Messages taken after hours will not be checked until the office is next open.

    Urgent Care

    If you are not pregnant or less than 20 weeks pregnant, please go to the nearest emergency department. Dr Mawdsley or an gynecologist colleague will be available if required.

    If you are more than 20 weeks pregnant, for concerns that cannot wait until the office is next open, please contact the IWK Health Centre at 902.470.6670 to speak to a nurse who will triage your call, or proceed directly to the Early labour Assessment Unit (ELAU) at the IWK Health Centre. You should call or go to the ELAU if you think your water has broken, you are having bleeding, your baby is not moving normally, or you are having pain or contractions. If you have any questions or concerns it is always best to call or be seen. Please identify yourself as Dr Mawdsley's patient. You will be assessed by Dr Mawdsley or obstetrical colleague.
